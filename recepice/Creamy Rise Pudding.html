Creamy Rise Pudding

This sweet rice pudding is thick, creamy, and utterly decadent.

Made with basic ingredients you likely already have on hand, this rice pudding recipe is the perfect comforting treat.

Ingredients

  • Rise
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Raisins
  • Butter
  • Vanilla

How to make Creamy Rise Pudding

  1. Cook the rise.
  2. Cook the cooked rice, some of the milk, sugar, and salt over medium heat until thick.
  3. Stir in the remaining milk, egg, and raisins. Cook for two more minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the butter and vanilla.
